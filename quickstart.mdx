## Welcome to Narrative Buddy 2

Learn how to harness the power of AI to transform your inputs into structured narrative statements efficiently.

### Getting Started with NB2

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="user-circle" title="Creating an Account">
    Getting started with NB2 is simple. Visit our website and click <a href="https://narrative-buddy.com/register" target="_blank">"Register"</a> to create an account. You can register using a username and password or opt for quick access via the "Sign in with Google" option. Once registered, you'll be ready to use NB2's chat function to generate narrative statements. To log out, click the icon at the bottom of the left-hand menu. For returning users, the <a href="https://narrative-buddy.com/login" target="_blank">"Login"</a> button is located at the top right of the homepage.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="chat-left-text" title="Crafting Your Narrative">
    NB2 is designed to convert your inputs into structured narratives. Whether it's freeform text or bullet points, simply enter your data, and NB2 will process it into a refined statement. Each Workshop in NB2 helps you develop and manage these narratives efficiently.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Using NB2 Effectively

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="pencil-square" title="Step 1: Input and Process">
    Enter your activities, achievements, or challenges in the chat interface of NB2. The AI processes these inputs and provides a structured narrative statement. Remember to refrain from entering classified or sensitive information.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="folder2-open" title="Step 2: Manage Your Statements">
    Developed narrative statements appear in the "Statements" section, where you can review and grade them. NB2 highlights each part of your narrative (Action, Impact, Result) in different colors for easy assessment and comparison.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
